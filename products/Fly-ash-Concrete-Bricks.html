Sustainable Fly Ash Concrete Bricks

Revolutionary eco-friendly building material made from fly ash - a byproduct of coal combustion in thermal power plants. Our fly ash bricks combine superior strength with environmental sustainability, manufactured using advanced compression technology without kiln firing, significantly reducing carbon emissions.

Superior performance characteristics across all parameters

Parameters Red Clay Bricks Fly Ash Bricks

Water Absorption 15-25% 10-14%
Uniformity Non-uniform shape Perfect uniformity
Breakage During Transport Up to 10% Less than 2%
