Cellular Foam Concrete (CLC)

Our Foam Concrete, also known as Cellular Lightweight Concrete (CLC), is an ultra-lightweight building material with exceptional thermal and acoustic insulation properties. Produced using advanced foaming technology with cement, fly ash, water, and specialized foaming agents for optimal performance.
